Comments

Easiest no-backend option is a GitHub-based widget (giscus). Configure it once and it works anywhere.

To enable giscus, replace the placeholder values in the embed snippet below: 

<script src="https://giscus.app/client.js"
  data-repo="YOUR_ORG/YOUR_REPO"
  data-repo-id="YOUR_REPO_ID"
  data-category="General"
  data-category-id="YOUR_CATEGORY_ID"
  data-mapping="pathname"
  data-theme="dark"
  crossorigin="anonymous"
  async></script>
Once configured, drop the giscus div/script into this page where you want comments to appear.
