Concept work

Three materially different homepage directions explored before the final RECPLACE selection.

The final homepage takes the first direction forward. It gave the clearest hierarchy, the strongest render-to-plan focal moment, and the most commercially credible balance between story and technical proof.

Open Final Homepage Open Leasing Page
Selected

Direction 01

Facade focus

A hero-led composition with the front rendering as the main focal point and a smaller plan inset that proves the project is grounded in documentation.

  • Best balance of memorability and restraint
  • Strongest separation between story, facts, and drawings
  • Most direct architectural focal point for the homepage

Direction 02

Editorial ledger

A lighter, more text-forward split layout built around a strong opening statement, quieter imagery, and a structured facts ledger.

  • Most corporate and text-led direction
  • Strong hierarchy, but less memorable hero moment
  • Felt credible, though less visually distinctive

Direction 03

Technical diptych

A more drawing-led homepage that pairs a large plan view with a smaller render strip and a stronger architectural-document reading.

  • Most architectural and drawing-centric
  • Clear for technical reviewers, weaker for first-time overview visitors
  • Risked underselling the building frontage as the opening impression
